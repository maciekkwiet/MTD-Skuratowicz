THE COMPANY

The company

Our company MTD Kaziemierz Skuratowicz is a traditional family-owned enterprise for transport and haulage, which was founded in 1981. We as a special company for complex transportation of oversized goods over the different transport carriers: air freight traffic, maritime traffic, inland waterway transport, and road and rail traffic, are a responsible service provider.

Through many years of international experience we are able to realize especially complex instructions.

Also our modern transport fleet gives us the possibility to transport your goods save and on schedule.

Every commission represents a new challenge for us and we strive for fulfilling every task adequately.

A satisfied client, reliability and flexibility are our fundamental values for a constant cooperation.

Those values you can also find between our employee. In our family-owned enterprise they get an modern workplace with enough space to create  their program of work and logistical projects by themselves.

This model guarantees every business partner a reliable and fundamental planning as a firm foundation for their missions.

We appreciate your interest and invite to be familiarized with the details of our offer. We would be pride to invite you in our customer circle.

Sincerely yours – Kazimierz Skuratowicz – IRT
